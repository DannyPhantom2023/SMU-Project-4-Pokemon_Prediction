Pokemon ML

Pokemon is an extremely popular series with properties ranging from television shows to card games, phone games and console games. There are entire stores dedicated to the franchise with worldwide locations. Fueling this massive empire is the ever expanding roster of pokemon, originally pocket monsters upon its original 1996 japanese release, the total now comes up to 1,015 unique pokemon! So we set out to create a predictive machine learning model that could tell us which pokemon would win in a battle. Our dataset included 801 pokemon which you can examine in our tableau dashboard, and then use our predictions page to put them up against one another.
